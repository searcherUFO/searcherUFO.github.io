A bizarre mix of explosive wisdom and reptilian chaos. Bombardino loves breakdancing in church ruins while yelling pasta-themed philosophy. Is he enlightened or completely lost? Both.

🎶 Trallalero Trallalà

Every word he says is sung. Every gesture turns into a music video. He once debated a parking meter using only falsetto. Trallalero is a one-man opera powered by glitchy Wi-Fi.

A ravioli revolutionary with five layers of sunglasses and no known address. He speaks in riddles made of pasta metaphors and dreams about rewriting physics using parmesan.

🐗 Gianni il Cinghiale

The wild boar of destiny. Convinced that traffic lights are a form of oppression, Gianni charges through reality snorting out conspiracy theories and demanding more salami.

👑 Principe del Caos

Prince of plastic chairs and chaos. He leads imaginary revolutions against walls and gives TED talks to pigeons. His crown is made of spaghetti forks and delusion.

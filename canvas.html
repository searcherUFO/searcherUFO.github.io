Canvas

Canvas is a very important part of game programming. Canvas is an area that can be adjusted using X and Y coordinates. Various shapes can be created inside the canvas. You can make these shapes move using additional code. Their movement can also be controlled using buttons and keys. The possibilities of the canvas is favorable for creating games, animations and computer drawings. There are several examples of canvas codes on this page.

A gradient is something like a shadow or fog. In canvas, a gradient is used by taking two or more colors and creating a kind of cluster. It can also be used as shading in the canvas.

This cube is made of two triangles and two rectangles. A gradient is also used here, which creates the impression of a shadow. This cube contains three colors and they are three shades of azure. #0275ab was used for the upper part of the cube, #001a26 and #026ea1 were used for the gradient.

A triangle looks like a simple shape, but its coordinates are quite complex. A triangle has three corners, and each corner has an X and Y coordinate, and the top of the triangle has two X and Y coordinates. And like any other shape, a gradient can be applied to a triangle.

Canvas is definitely a very important element. In addition to canvas, there is SVG. But SVG is not as reliable and not as good for creating games as canvas. But SVG is also relatively simpler than canvas.

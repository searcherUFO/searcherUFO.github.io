TOP 3 Ubisoft mobile games

Ubisoft is one of the biggest game makers and of course they have also been involved in creating mobile games.Ubisoft makes lot of great games but this is the Top 3 of their best mobile games ever.

1.Hungry Shark Evolution

Hungry Shark Evolution is very good game.Map in this game is really big and have a lot of easter eggs.One of this easter eggs is Kempy fish with human face.This game is offcourse mobile and playable without WI-FI connection.Hungry Shark Evolution is created by Ubisoft studio and studio FGOL(Future Games Of London). Story of game You are hungry shark in the sea.in the game you start with a Reef Shark which has a lot of disadvantages, two of them are that it can't eat jellyfish and it has Low Health. when you collect enough money you can buy a better shark like Tiger Shark or Megalodon.

2.Hungry Shark World

Hungry Shark World is similiar to Hungry Shark Evolution but Hungry Shark World is more sophisticated because it has more maps, better graphics and controls.There are also more easter eggs and Kempy is also here, but in the form of a yetti in the Antarctica level.In Hungry Shark World, it is easier to collect gems. In Hungry Shark World, you can also collect pearls in Extinction Levels. You can then buy sharks from the DNA series with pearls.

3.Hungry Shark Arena

Hungry Shark Arena is an online multiplayer game that is quite similar to other Hungry Shark games, but the game has different graphics. In the game, you have a shark that you fight against other sharks. The last shark left in the arena wins. You can gradually improve the sharks in this game.You can play this Hungry Shark game on the Ubisoft Nano website.

This was a list of the best Hungry Shark games, of course there are more but this was a list of the most played ones. You can also check out our other games articles.
